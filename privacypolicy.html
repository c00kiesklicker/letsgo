Log Data

Whenever you visit our Service, our servers collect automated information that your browser sends to us that is called Log Data. This Log Data may include information such as your computer's Internet Protocol ("IP") address, browser version, pages of our Service that you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those pages, and other statistics.

This information is solely used by CookieClickerFree for maintenance purposes ie. balancing server loads or resolving server errors. Our Log Data is wiped regularly.

Cookies

Cookies are files containing small amounts of data commonly used to store user data across browser sessions. These are sent to your browser from the website that you visit and are stored on your device's hard drive.

Parts of the CookieClickerFree website use cookies for various purposes, mainly to store save data or settings for our games. In the future, we may also use cookies to store login data, ie. as part of online multiplayer games. You have the option to either accept or refuse these cookies, and know when a cookie is being sent to your computer. If you choose to refuse our cookies, you may not be able to use some portions of our Service to their fullest. (Notably, our game Cookie Clicker requires cookies to function.)

We also allow third-party companies to set cookies on your device as parts of services they provide such as analytics and ad. See the sections Service Providers and Ads for more information.

Further information on our cookie usage as well as links detailing how to manage cookies can be found on our Cookie Consent Policy.
